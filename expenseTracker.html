Expense Tracker App

This was a project I started back in June 2020 when I first learned React-JS. I was the sole creator/developer for the project.

This tracker stored the sample data for the expenses and incomes to track record of the whole day budget.

It is to tell the clients with the quick view of their balances at the last of the day.
